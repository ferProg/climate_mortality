## Spine (siempre)

* **Deads weekly**: sale del `weekly_master` leyendo `data/raw/deaths/ine_35178.csv` (tu `load_deaths_from_raw`).
* Eso define el calendario de semanas (lunes ISO) y es el “spine” del merge.

## Fuentes weekly que se enganchan al spine

1. **Weather (AEMET estación)**

   * `python -m src.ingests.aemet_station_daily_to_weekly ...`
   * Output: `data/processed/<island>/weather/weather_weekly_<code>_<startyear>_<endyear>.parquet`

2. **Air quality (Excels por estación)**

   * `python -m src.ingests.air_quality_canarias_station_excels_to_pm ...`
   * Output: `data/processed/<island>/air_quality/air_quality_weekly_<code>_<startyear>_<endyear>.parquet`
   * (y si faltan semanas: lo calendarizas con tu notebook “parches”)

3. **CAP avisos (isla)**

   * Desde dataset canarias en `data/interim/cap/canarias/year_onset=...`
   * `python -m src.ingests.cap.extract_canarias_avisos_by_island --isla "<isla>" --start ... --end ...`
   * Output: `data/processed/<island>/cap/cap_weekly_<code>_<startyear>_<endyear>.parquet`

4. **Visibility airports (1 aeropuerto por isla)**

   * `python -m src.ingests.calima_airports.run_island_pipeline ...`
   * Output: `data/processed/<island>/visibility/visibility_weekly_<code>_<startyear>_<endyear>.parquet`
