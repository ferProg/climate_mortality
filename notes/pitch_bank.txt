45s version

I’ve been working on a data project analysing how heat, Saharan dust events and air quality may relate to weekly mortality in Tenerife. I brought together official datasets from different sources, cleaned and merged them, and built a structured weekly dataset for analysis. The project helped me develop practical skills in data cleaning, feature engineering, exploratory analysis and producing reliable, reporting-ready data. What I like most about it is that it combines messy real-world data with a meaningful public health question.
